About Koalgo

Koalgo's mission is to encourage students to play with language using code. In Korean keompyuteo kodeu means "computer code" and algo sip-eohaneun geos means "inquisitive". About the Creator

Koalgo is created by Karen Schwarze, a San Francisco Bay Area native and former English teacher. She has a B.A in English Education and an M.A. English. She believes that coding belongs in English class and is deeply passionate about making coding education fun and accessible to English teachers and students.
