About Koalgo

Koalgo is built on code exploration. In Korean keompyuteo kodeu means "computer code" and algo sip-eohaneun means "inquisitive". As a brand Koalgo seeks to encourage people to be curious and experimental about how code applies to our lives, including our literary lives. Put together the Korean words for "computer code" and "inquisitive" and you have Koalgo. Koalgo runs on HTML5Up, Trinket, Visual Studio Code, and [hosting service].

About the Creator

Koalgo is created by Karen Schwarze, a San Francisco Bay Area native and former teacher. She has a B.A in English Education and a M.A. English. She believes that coding belongs in English class and is deeply passionate about making coding education fun and accessible to English teachers and students.
